  • Lamb Nihari

How to make Lamb Nihari:

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add lamb pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  4. Add spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder) and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add water to cover the meat and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 2-3 hours until the meat is tender.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix wheat flour with water to make a smooth paste. Add this to the pot to thicken the gravy.
  7. Cook for another 15-20 minutes until the gravy thickens. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and ginger slices.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice.
